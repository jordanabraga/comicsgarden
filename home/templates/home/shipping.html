Shipping & Collection Policy

Shipping

At Comics Garden, we strive to get your orders to you as quickly and efficiently as possible. Here’s what you need to know about our shipping policy:

Shipping Methods

  • Standard Shipping: Delivered within 5-7 business days.
  • Express Shipping: Delivered within 2-3 business days. Additional charges apply.
  • Overnight Shipping: Delivered the next business day. Additional charges apply.

Collection

For those who prefer to pick up their orders in person, we offer a convenient collection service from our store. Here’s how it works:

Collection Location

You can collect your order from our store located at:

Comics Garden
123 Comic Street
Downtown, CA 12345
USA

Collection Hours

Orders can be collected during our store opening hours:

  • Monday - Friday: 11:00 AM - 8:00 PM
  • Saturday: 10:00 AM - 9:00 PM
  • Sunday: 12:00 PM - 6:00 PM

Collection Process

Once your order is ready for collection, you will receive an email notification with a collection reference number. Please bring this email and a valid ID when you come to collect your order.

Returns and Exchanges

All items collected in-store are subject to our standard return and exchange policy. For more information, please contact us.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our shipping and collection policies, please do not hesitate to contact us.
